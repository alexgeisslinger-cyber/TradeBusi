What We Do

SignalsLayered is a Bitcoin market intelligence platform that aggregates and analyzes multiple layers of market data into a single, actionable signal score. We combine sentiment indicators, prediction markets, macroeconomic data, and on-chain metrics to give traders and investors a clear picture of the current Bitcoin market environment.

Our proprietary Signal Score distills complex market data into one number — making it easy to understand where the market stands at any given moment, without needing to analyze dozens of sources yourself.
